BACoN Project

Introduction

BACoN is a group project for course KIK-LG211 Building NLP Applications and it is named after its four members: Baiyi, Arthur, Chao and Nikolay.

BACoN is to collect latest news in medical field by scraping information from the internet.

Current progress

Currently, we are working on a web crawler that can scrape news from Science Daily and Medical News Today, and store them in a database.
